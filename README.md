# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report – 2022

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the **annual sales data of Vrinda Store for the year 2022** using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance in order to help the store make **data-driven decisions for growth in 2023**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To create an interactive and visual sales dashboard that provides insights into:

* Monthly sales and order trends
* Customer demographics (age and gender)
* Order status distribution
* Top-performing states and product categories
* Sales performance across different channels

---

## 🛠️ What I Did

* Cleaned and prepared raw sales data for analysis
* Created multiple **PivotTables** to analyze sales, orders, customers, and regions
* Designed interactive **PivotCharts** for better data visualization
* Used **Slicers** to filter data dynamically by month, channel, and category
* Ensured all PivotTables use a single data source for proper slicer connectivity
* Formatted numbers (e.g., millions format) for clear readability
* Built a complete **Excel dashboard** with insights and conclusions

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

* Compared sales and orders using a combined chart
* Identified the month with the highest sales and orders
* Analyzed purchases by men and women
* Examined order status such as delivered, cancelled, returned, and refunded
* Identified the top 10 states contributing to total sales
* Studied the relationship between age group and gender
* Determined the highest-performing sales channel
* Found the highest-selling product category

---

## 📊 Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* PivotTables & PivotCharts
* Slicers
* Data formatting & sorting
* Dashboard design principles

---

## ✅ Outcome

The dashboard provides a **clear and interactive view of Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales performance**. It helps identify key customer segments, high-performing regions, and popular product categories. These insights can be used to improve marketing strategies, optimize inventory planning, and drive higher sales in the future.

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates the effective use of Excel for data analysis and business reporting. By transforming raw data into meaningful insights, the dashboard supports informed decision-making and showcases practical data analytics skills.
